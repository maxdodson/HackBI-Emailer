Dear teachers,

HackBI, Bishop Ireton High School's annual hackathon, is back for the third year! We invite you and your students to join us on December 7-8!

HackBI is a 24-hour coding event where students in middle and high school are immersed in the exciting field of computer science. Surrounded by programming resources, students learn new skills, create projects, and can win prizes for what they come up with! HackBI is a great resource for both beginners and experienced programmers, as there will be workshops and prize categories for both! Additionally, students will be able to build and control robots as well as win prizes in raffles and by solving cybersecurity challenges!



If your students are interested, please have them visit hackbi.org to register and learn more. Please see the attached flyer and video for more information.

If you or other adults are interested in volunteering, we would greatly appreciate it! To learn more, visit hackbi.org/volunteer.

Don't hesitate to reach out if you have any questions! You may reply to this email or contact Terri Kelly, teacher & organizer, directly at kellyt@bishopireton.org.

Thanks! We hope to see you and your students at HackBI!

-The HackBI Team
