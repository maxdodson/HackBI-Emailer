Hi ,

Thank you for registering for HackBI III! We can’t wait to see you there!

In preparation for December 7, we have some important information for all attendees. Please read everything below carefully.


Forms

Please remember that without properly completed forms, you will not be able to attend HackBI (no exceptions).

You can find and submit forms at hackbi.org/register under Students.

Please note that one of these forms must be signed by an administrator at your school. You should turn this in to your administration now to ensure that it is signed before HackBI. Again, you must have this form completed to attend.


Attendance

If you are unable to attend HackBI, please let us know as soon as possible.

Only students who have been approved to leave early and return may do so. Those not approved to leave early will not be allowed to return or receive prizes. If you are planning to arrive late or leave early, please let us know as soon as possible.

Please bring a student ID or other form of identification. This is necessary for registration and any possible use of the hardware lab. If you do not have one, please let us know.


Volunteers

Adult volunteers are greatly needed to be chaperones at HackBI. Volunteers can sign up here.

If you know anyone who may be interested in volunteering (parents, teachers, etc.), please pass the information along.


Workshops

For your best experience at HackBI, please fill out this form to indicate interest in our workshops.

This information will help us better schedule workshops and offer additional resources.


Software

Some workshops may require you to install certain software. If you plan to attend the following workshops, please download the software at home so workshops can begin on time.

Workshop Software
Java JDK
jGrasp
Python Python 3.7+
Alexa Skills with AWS Python 3.7+
GitHub Git
Google Cloud API Google Account
Python

Everyone should have a code editor installed. Free ones include Sublime Text, VS Code, and Atom.


Safety

Student health and safety is of utmost importance during HackBI. Accordingly, rules such as no weapons, no possession or use of controlled substances (including, but not limited to, vape devices, marijuana, alcohol, drugs or drug paraphernalia), and treating others with respect will be enforced.

Please remember that the school retains the right to inspect items that are brought onto the premises and take appropriate action if rules are violated. All attendees must adhere to Bishop Ireton rules and expectations.


Allergies

If you haven't notified us of any allergies you have, please do so by responding to this email.


Medicine

EpiPens and inhalers may be kept on your person.

All other medicine must be brought in its original container and must be accompanied with this form.


Arrival & Parking

Registration begins at 9 AM on Saturday, December 7. You may be dropped off at the lower entrance. If you're driving yourself, there is parking available at Bishop Ireton for the duration of HackBI. You can't leave during HackBI to retrieve belongings left in your car, so bring everything when you arrive.

If you choose to park off the Ireton campus, be aware that parking regulations are strictly enforced in the area.

Everyone should use the lower entrance near the parking lot to enter the school.


Please let us know if you have any questions. You may reply to this email.

See you at HackBI!
